Our Story

Built on a simple idea: flying should feel effortless

Stackly began with three aircraft and a single domestic route, founded by a group of aviation engineers who believed booking and boarding could be far less stressful than it was.

Today that idea has grown into a full-service airline connecting six continents, but the goal hasn't changed — get people where they're going, comfortably and on time.

Every process we build, from fare search to boarding, is measured against that original promise.
