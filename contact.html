Common questions

How do I change or cancel my flight? add

Go to Manage Booking on our site, or contact our support team with your booking reference.

What is the baggage allowance for my fare? add

Allowance depends on your cabin class and route — check your fare details at checkout or in your confirmation email.

How can I check in for my flight? add

Online check-in opens 48 hours before departure through our website or mobile app.

Do you offer refunds on cancelled flights? add

Refund eligibility depends on your fare type; flexible fares qualify for a full refund to your original payment method.
