Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions travellers ask before and after booking.

How do I change my flight date?

add

Go to Manage Booking, enter your reference number, and select a new date. Fare difference and change fees, if any, will be shown before you confirm.

What is the baggage allowance?

add

Standard economy fares include 20kg checked baggage and 7kg cabin baggage. Extra baggage can be added during or after booking.

How long do refunds take?

add

Refunds are processed within 7 to 10 business days and credited back to your original payment method.

Can I check in online?

add

Yes, online check-in opens 48 hours before departure and closes 2 hours prior to your flight.
